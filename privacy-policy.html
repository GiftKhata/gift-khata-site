01 Who runs GiftKhata?

GiftKhata is a personal side project maintained by a small group of volunteers. There is no registered company, no registered address, and no legal entity behind this application. When we say "we" or "us" in this policy we mean the volunteer contributors to this project.

Questions? Email us at support@giftkhata.com.

📋

02 What data we collect

From your sign-in provider

We use OAuth 2.0 (Google, Facebook, Apple, LinkedIn). When you sign in, your provider shares your name, email address, and profile photo with us. We store only these three fields. We never see or store your password.

Data you enter yourself

Everything you type into the app — gift records, event details, person names, phone numbers, cities, family tree data — is stored in our database linked to your account. You own this data. We don't read it, analyse it, or use it for any purpose other than showing it back to you.

Basic usage logs

Our hosting provider (Firebase / Google Cloud) automatically logs standard server request data: IP address, browser type, and timestamps. We do not add any additional analytics, session recording, or behavioural tracking on top of this.

🚫

03 What we do NOT collect

  • Passwords (handled entirely by your sign-in provider)
  • Payment or financial account information (the app is free)
  • Location data / GPS
  • Contacts from your phone
  • Advertising identifiers or cross-site tracking cookies
  • Sensitive personal data beyond what you voluntarily enter
⚙️

04 How we use your data

We use your data for exactly one purpose: to run the app for you.

  • Show your gift ledger, events, persons, and family tree
  • Sync your data across your devices when you sign in
  • Let you export or delete your data on request

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not use it to train AI models. We do not analyse it for business insights.

👁️

05 Who can see your data

Your data is private to your account. Nobody else can see it through the app. The volunteer maintainers have administrative access to the database for the sole purposes of keeping the service running and responding to support requests.

We use the following third-party services to run the app. Each receives only the minimum data required:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and database hosting. Your data sits on Google's servers, subject to their Privacy Policy.
  • Your sign-in provider (Google, Apple, Facebook, or LinkedIn) — for authentication only.

We will share your data with law enforcement only if required by a valid court order under Indian law.

🛡️

06 Your rights

You can, at any time:

  • Export your data — Settings → Account → Export (or email us)
  • Delete your account and all data — email us at support@giftkhata.com and we will delete everything within 30 days
  • Correct your data — edit it directly in the app, or ask us to correct it
  • Ask what we hold — email us and we'll tell you exactly what's stored for your account

These rights are consistent with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India).

🔒

07 Security

We take reasonable precautions: data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest on Firebase's infrastructure. Authentication is handled by your OAuth provider — we never handle passwords directly.

That said, no internet service is 100% secure. Use the app at your own risk, and please don't store anything critically sensitive (like financial account numbers or government ID numbers) in the notes fields.

👶

08 Children

GiftKhata is intended for adults (18+). We don't knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has signed up, email us and we'll delete the account.

🍪

09 Cookies

We use only essential session cookies set by Firebase for keeping you signed in. No advertising cookies, no third-party tracking pixels.

🔄

10 Changes to this policy

If we make material changes we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where possible, notify you in-app. The latest version is always at this URL.

📬

11 Contact

This is a community project so please be patient. For privacy questions or data deletion requests, email support@giftkhata.com. We'll do our best to respond within a week.
