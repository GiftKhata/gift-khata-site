How It Works

Up and running in minutes

Simple, intuitive flow designed for any family. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use GiftKhata.

1
Add your event or function

Create an event with name, date, and type (wedding, birthday, housewarming, Christmas, Eid, graduation…). Unlimited events — past and upcoming.

2
Log gifts received

For each gift enter the giver's name, gift type (cash / item / voucher), amount or value, and notes. Takes under 30 seconds per entry.

3
Add people to your network

Create contact profiles for relatives and friends. Link them to gift entries across multiple events — automatically building their full history.

4
Record gifts you give

When you attend someone's celebration, log what you gave. GiftKhata shows both sides — gifts received and given — for complete clarity.

5
Track and review anytime

Open any event or person's profile to see totals, types, and amounts. Use insights to plan future gifting thoughtfully.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GiftKhata.

What is GiftKhata and who is it for?

GiftKhata is a free digital gift ledger for families around the world. It replaces scattered paper lists with a smart digital register to track all gifts received and given at weddings, birthdays, housewarmings, Christmas, Eid, graduations and every family occasion.

Is GiftKhata free to use?

Yes — GiftKhata is completely free to get started. Add unlimited events, people, and gift entries at no cost. Premium features may be introduced later, but the core gift tracking will always remain free.

Which gift types can I track in GiftKhata?

You can track cash gifts, gift vouchers (Amazon, John Lewis, local brands), physical items (silverware, appliances, jewellery) in any currency. Digital transfers (bank, UPI, PayPal, Venmo) coming soon.

Can I track gifts I have given to others?

Absolutely. GiftKhata maintains a complete two-sided ledger per person — showing both what you received from them and what you gave at their functions. This helps you plan return-gifting thoughtfully and fairly.

Can multiple family members use the same GiftKhata?

Yes. GiftKhata supports family sharing, so your spouse or parents can add gift entries for the same event from different phones and everything stays in sync automatically.

Does GiftKhata work offline?

Yes. You can add gift entries even without internet connection. Your data syncs automatically once you are back online — so you never miss a gift entry, whether you're at a village wedding or a city reception.

Is my gifting data safe and private?

Yes. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. GiftKhata never shares your personal gifting records with any third party. Only you and family members you invite can see your khata.
