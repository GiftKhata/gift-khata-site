How It Works

Up and running in minutes

Simple, intuitive flow — no training needed. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use GiftKhata.

1
Add your event or function
Create an event with its name, date, and type (wedding, birthday, anniversary, etc.). You can have unlimited events — past and upcoming.
2
Log gifts received
For each gift, enter the giver's name, gift type (cash / item / voucher), amount or value, and any notes. Takes less than 30 seconds per entry.
3
Add people to your network
Create contact profiles for relatives and friends. Once added, link them to any gift entry across multiple events — automatically building their ledger history.
4
Record gifts you give
When you attend someone's function, log what you gave. Your khata shows both sides — received and given — for complete clarity.
5
Track and review anytime
Open any event or person's profile to see total gifts, types, and amounts. Use insights to plan future gifting thoughtfully.
